About Us

The morning fix grab and go coffee bakes is cozy corner in Cape Town created with students and professionals in mind. We know mornings can be busy and draining so our goal is to offer you the perfect balance between work life and convenience. Whether you’re rushing to class or the office or just need a quick pick-me-up, we’ve got you covered. From our freshly baked pastries to our delicious coffees, every item is made with love and care to start your day with purpose and productivity. Your day deserves a good start, let us be your fix.
